The standards and beliefs of our generation

​ Firstly I want to express my apology for using the word “we” to represent the generation of my peers in such a hasty way, and I know it’s rather incorrect to do that which may have the intention to draw people’s attention, but still hope it will get empathy from someone. I would use the expression “our generation” in several times in the following paragraph, you guys don’t need to plug yourself into the scene, just regard it as a normal reading.

​ Currently I talk about “this generation”, which especially means the students who are in undergraduate. It’s easy to find that university is different from junior high school and senior high school, meanwhile you can still feel that university is the continue of senior high school. At least these contradictory ideas often come up in my daily life.

​ The so-called difference means university seems not like junior high school or senior high school, which the only evaluation standard is your grade. It’s quite a lot different standard in university. In my view, no matter the imagination of the college during senior high school or the imagination of the future as a undergraduate student, I wouldn’t be constrained by certain standard to do the self-evaluation, I know it’s a blasphemy to the gold four years of my life.

​ However, if you regard the university as the continue of the senior high school, there’s nothing wrong with that. Actually it’s reality, everyone has the choice to chase their dreams and to make mistakes, to fight for a different life. It’s the freedom of the student, also it’s the freedom of a person. But human is, after all, the creature trapped in system. It’s quite hard for someone to keep his nose clean and go his own way in such an environment. Especially when there’s lots of people take the same evaluation which creates by one who has reached some success, you cannot promise that you’ll not be imperceptibly indoctrinated by others.

​ Usually I wonder what’s the problem which makes me feel that the unversity is just the continue of the senior high school. Probably due to the possible colorful life we could pursue no more than the following types: get the postgraduate recommendation, prepare for the postgraduate examination, get a job, go abroad for study or just doing nothing. Personally I still believe that even though lots of people pursue the same target, the scenery along the way varies. It’s a gain for one but as the society and the school which have become streamlined, the evaluation standard of them wouldn’t care about these along side scenery, the only thing they gonna do is selecting quantitative awards and competency indicators to put the tag to everyone under these environments. If you compare the universities today with the universities of 20 years ago, the standard is more likly the chains which locked the people. Postgraduate is not the only way out from university as I have listed before, however, the evaluation standard of the university is only for postgraduate.

​ When the deep research and posting paper could be the extra points in getting various awards, when the students flock into the laboratory and the only purpose for them is to add a few contents to their resume but not inspired by the interest of doing research, it’s the truth that the standard of university is biased somehow. Seems like the whole society is anxious, when someone has not decided the future road yet during the undergraduate, the school is in a hurry to set the standard of measuring someone’s excellence by scientific achievement. They do not have a good idea about living now but pushing the students to pursue the meaningless things, inevitably it will make outsiders laugh.

​ I don’t think it’s the problem caused by our generation. When we can only be the player of the game rather than being the rule maker, no matter how abnormal one person becomes during the process of the game, we shouldn’t blame this fault on the those participants. Often I asked myself, do we really need to hurry? In a hurry to think for the future, in a hurry to schedule everything, they have scheduled their life in one hundred years even they are not thirty years old. I don’t know it’s a good or bad thing. I cannot tell whether they are mature or not, but if everyone is expecting the next procedure in their current situation, I don’t think it’s the thing we should applaud and it’s the thing the whole society should flock into and admire for.

​ Here comes the second topic, the faith of our generation. Our generation seems to be different from all other generations, we hold the a seemingly neutral mind and horizon to treat things. Different from those who believe in all kind of religions or have faith in Party, we believe nothing, in our eyes, everyone could be pulled from the shrines, the higher the one is being regarded, the hard he’ll fall when he did something wrong.

​ Faith is often connected with the word “standard”. When something becomes the “most” of some part or field among the public, then it will become a standard, become some of people’s faith, they are the words which come up together. According to this logic, when society tends to set the certain standard, this standard may also become the faith to some people. What’s more, the difference between the “faith” and “standard” is that the “faith” is connected with the word “fanaticism”.

​ I am afraid I was going crazy, I know that most of the opinion on standard is the social appendage. And when I become a fanatics, I tend to regard some of the social standard as the faith or belief. I have to say that I don’t think some of the social standard is wrong, meanwhile I believe that it needs to be considered seriously by oneself to raise the social standard to the personal faith.

​ I hope myself to avoid trapping into the fanaticism situation, because I want to fight. Many people also want to fight. There are two conditions in fighting, one is so-called the attitude of fighting, which represents the insight towards the society. I think I should belong to the second type, the act of protest. In my view the difference between the second one and the first one is that someone who owns the protest attitude will go to extremes, when the person who holds the protest idea towards everything, he will easliy go to irrational situation. However, someone who has the act of protest will take the protest attitude towards the specific things.

​ As I have mentioned before, I want to be on the edge of the system or society, which may help me avoid trapping into fanaticism or having faith to the standard set by school. In short is to take the critical insight to treat everything. But when we trapped into the fanaticism, critical insight could be harmful, we would become the person who only criticize those who disdain that standard, it’s quite rude and absurd. Man should never have the idea that his own choice should become the whole human’s choice and regard his own life as the most correct life, if someone hold this idea, he should be the terminal fanatics.
